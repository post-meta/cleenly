## CONTENT STRUCTURE

### Hero Section - Entity Definition
Clear statement of what CLEENLY is:
- House cleaning booking platform
- Serves Seattle and Greater Eastside
- Connects customers with vetted cleaning professionals
- Simple online booking system
- Platform (not a cleaning company)

### Why We Built This
Story-based content addressing common pain points:
- "Starting at $99" that becomes $180
- No idea who's coming to clean
- Phone tag to book

### How CLEENLY Works

**For Customers:**
1. Tell us about your place
2. See your price range
3. Browse cleaners
4. Pick a time
5. Book

**For Cleaners:**
- Set availability and rates
- Get customers without marketing
- Keep most of earnings

1. Price on screen = price you pay
2. You know who's coming
3. Problems get fixed
4. Cleaners get paid fairly
